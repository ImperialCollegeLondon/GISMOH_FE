Help for Positive and Risk Patients

This is a list of patients that are in the hospital that have MRSA or have had MRSA previosuly. Patients with red icons are colonised, those with blue icons have previously been colonised. A patient with a blue background is currently selected.

Use this Panel:

  • Click on a patient to see who else has carried a similar isolate

Help for Related Isolates Panel

The blue circle is the patient currently selected, yellow circles represent patients who with similar isolates, red triangles represent patienst who have similar isolates who have also spent time on the same ward as the patient

Use this Panel:

  • Click on a patient to select them and view their diagram

Help for Timeline

The timeline shows where patients have been in the hospital, episodes (stays on a ward) are gray by default, turning blue for the currently selected patient (the patient with a tick by their number), and yellow for any stay that have overlapped with that patient. Patients can be "locked" to the top of the list by clicking on their row. Locked patients numbers' appear in bold, and are also signified by a padlock. Green Circles are swabs that do not show MRSA carriage, red triangles are swabs that show MRSA carriage

Use this Panel:

  • Click on a patient to lock the patient to lock or unlock them
  • Roll over a positive swab to highlight other swabs which show carriage of a related isolate.
